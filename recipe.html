Recipe #1: No-Bake Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes to cool off
  • Serves: 12 to 24
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar, Oleo, Salt, Milk, and Cocoa 2cups, 1 stick, 1/4tsp, 1/2cup, and 3tbsp

Step 1: stir abouve ingredietns until boiling cook for 1 minute longer with no stirring and stove off.

Step 2: add 1tsp of Vanilla and 1/2cup of Peanut butter to mixture

Step 3: then add 3 cups of Oats until thicken

Step 4: drop by teaspoon onto wax paper

Step 5: let them harden for awhile, possibly 30 minutes

Step 6: then eat!!!!

Source: from my mom's recipe book

Recipe #2: Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 40 minutes(5-10 minutes if still wet or moist)
  • Serves: a whole cake
Ingredients Quantity
cocoa, eggs, self-rising flour, sugar, butter, and milk 2tbsp, 2, 1cup, 1cup, 1/2 stick(melted), 1/2cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.

Step 2:Place all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 3:Beat with an electric beater until the mixture is smooth.

Step 4: Pour into grease and base lined (baking paper on the bottom) cake tin.

Step 5: Bake for approximately 40 minutes at 325°F (160°C) or until cooked when tested.

Step 6: Add frosting, sprinkles, and any other sorts of decoration.

Source: Simple Cake
